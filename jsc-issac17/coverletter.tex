\noindent Cover letter for the revision of {\bf Block-Krylov techniques in the context of sparse-FGLM algorithms}, by Hyun, Neiger, Rahkooy, Schost.

\bigskip

We wish to thank the reviewer for their remarks, which we tried to follow
to the best of our ability.

In hindsight, we agree that the paper was too verbose at places, so we
significantly reduced its length, without sacrificing contents. As
suggested by the reviewer, certain proofs that were almost identical
to existing ones were replaced by references to the literature. A few
statements about linearly recurrent matrix sequences are now proved in
appendix, to streamline Section 2. In the submitted version, Sections
2 and 3 were respectively 10 and 8 pages long; they are now roughly 5,
resp.\ 4 pages long.

A few other minor corrections were made in the paper (mostly
typos). Altogether, we hope you will find the new version more
readable (we certainly do). Thank you again for your suggestions.
